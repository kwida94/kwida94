# Hi, I'm Karl Wida

I'm a self-taught data analyst based in Muncie, Indiana, building my skills through real projects and hands-on learning. By day I work in sales and customer service at a financial institution — a role that's given me a genuine appreciation for the data behind business decisions. Increasingly, that's where I want to spend my time: understanding the *why* behind the numbers.

I specialize in **SQL**, **Power BI**, and **Excel / Power Query**, and I'm focused on turning messy, real-world data into clear, actionable insights.
